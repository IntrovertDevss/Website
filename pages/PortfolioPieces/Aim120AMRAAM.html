AIM 120 - AMRAAM

Price: $0.00

This is a model of the AIM 120b AMRAAM Missile. It was modeled based on the real thing, with dimensions as accurate as possible. It's meant to depict the Missile in flight, not maneuvering. The model includes baked textures that are 8k resolution with a diffuse, roughness, and metallic map. This project took around a month, with tons of procrastinating, and terrible efficiency.
